

## Add Birthday Image

Copy the uploaded birthday image to `public/images/birthday.jpg` and update its `objectPosition` in `ServicesSection.tsx`.

The image is portrait-oriented with the main subject (cake, balloons, teddy bears) centered. An `objectPosition` of `center center` should work well.

### Changes

1. **Copy** `user-uploads://Birthday_1.jpg` to `public/images/birthday.jpg`
2. **Update** the Birthday Parties service entry in `ServicesSection.tsx` — set `objectPosition: "center center"` (the subject fills the frame evenly)
